Jing Liu (            
)

Senior Software Engineer
Natural Language Processing at Baidu Inc.


I am a senior software engineer at Baidu NLP since December 2017. Before that, I was a researcher at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) from September 2014 to December 2017. I obtained Ph.D. degree in computer science from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) under the supervision of Prof. Hsiao-Wuen Hon (MSRA & HIT), Prof. Ting Liu (HIT) and Dr. Chin-Yew Lin (MSRA) in September 2014. I participated in the joint Ph.D. program of HIT and MSRA, and I was working as a long-term research intern at MSRA from July 2009 to September 2014. I received M.S. degree in computer science from HIT under the supervision of Prof. Yu Zhang and Prof. Ting Liu in July 2009. I obtained B.S. degree in computer science from Xidian University (XDU) in July 2007.
